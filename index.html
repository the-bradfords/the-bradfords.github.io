The nBradfords

As I've been researching my family history, I was dumbfounded at the sheer size of this family. There are several different lines of Bradfords that date back to the Mayflower. From my research so far, I don't think my line of Bradfords descended from the Mayflower. But this geneology study is as ever changing as the Bradford family dynamics themselves. I am hoping to uncover the origins of my Bradford line throughout this ongoing project.
